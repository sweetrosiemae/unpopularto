Artists

Greatness doesn't have to be popular.

Unpopular started as a collaborative idea from two Toronto creatives - Keston & Rosie aka Kesteeze & Sweet Rosie Mae. Both are entrepreneurs who come from the world of dance, entertainment, and running their own small businesses. With over a decade of independant hustling, the duo are certainly not strangers to how often great ideas, artists, and small businesses are overlooked. With that in mind - and also just a love for creating something new - the pair came up with Unpopular. Unpopular is a brand that represents ideas, creatives, and businesses that succeed independantly in a large-scale, corporate-controlled world.

Divo

Divo grew up in Toronto and reps the East York area. A dancer, performer, choreographer, stylist, and song writer - Divo is truly a multifaceted artist that sees no limits when it comes to creating. Sharing his artistic energy online, he also is the owner and operator of an independant social media company @versatile.child Divo is versatile, dynamic, and always ready to entertain - he always brings the drammmaaaa hunny!
